Escalation Procedure

First Line Support

The first line of support handles initial customer inquiries and issues. Their responsibilities include:

  • Gathering information and troubleshooting using standard procedures
  • Resolving straightforward, well-documented problems

Escalation to Second Level Support

Escalation to second-level support occurs under the following circumstances:

  • The issue is complex or requires specialized knowledge
  • Initial troubleshooting steps have been exhausted
  • The customers issue is high priority or critical
  • First-line support is unsure of the solution or requires guidance
  • Technical expertise beyond first-line support training is needed

Escalation to Third Level Support

Escalation to third-level support is necessary when:

  • The issue requires advanced technical expertise or architectural knowledge
  • Second-level support is unable to resolve the issue
  • High-priority or critical issues require immediate attention
  • There is a potential security vulnerability or system compromise
  • An unknown or new issue requires research and development
