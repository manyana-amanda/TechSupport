Troubleshooting Procedure Questions

Initial Questions

  • What issue are you experiencing?
  • How did you encounter the issue? (Steps leading up to the problem)
  • Can you describe the error message? (If any)

Gathering Information

  • What software or application is affected? (Name, Version)
  • What device and operating system are you using?
  • Are you using any third-party plugins or extensions?
  • Have you had any recent updates? (Hardware, Software, Configuration)

Isolation

  • Can you reproduce the issue? (Consistency)
  • Does the issue occur consistently or intermittently? (Frequency)
  • Have you tried using a different account?

Configuration and Environment

  • Have you checked for loose connections or hardware issues? (Physical Inspection)
  • Is your device up-to-date? (Latest Version)
  • Are there any conflicts with other programs or devices? (Compatibility)

Advanced Troubleshooting

  • Can you provide logs or screenshots? (Diagnostic data)
  • Have you tried restarting or resetting the device/application? (Basic troubleshooting)
  • Have you checked for firmware updates for any hardware device?
  • Are there any custom settings or configuration? (Non-default settings)
